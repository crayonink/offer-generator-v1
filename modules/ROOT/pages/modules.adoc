== Selection engine (`bom/selectors/`)

`selection_engine.py` orchestrates catalog-part selection (burner, gas train, AGR,
blower, air duct, butterfly valve, rotary joint, HPU). Individual selectors:
`encon_burner`, `ng_gas_train`, `agr_selector`, `blower_selector`, `air_duct_selector`,
`air_valve_selector`, `rotary_joint_selector`, `orifice_plate_selector`,
`gas_regulator_selector`, `hpu_selector`.

== BOM builders (`bom/*_builder.py`)

Each builder turns selected equipment + parameters into priced `_row(...)` line items
tagged with `MEDIA`, `ITEM NAME`, `REFERENCE`, `QTY`, `MAKE`, `UNIT PRICE`, `TOTAL`.

[cols="2,3", options="header"]
|===
| Builder | Covers
| `vlph_builder.py` | Vertical & (shared) ladle preheater BOM
| `hlph_builder.py` | Horizontal ladle preheater BOM
| `recup_builder.py` | Recuperator BOM
| `regen_builder.py` | Regenerative burner BOM (scaffolding)
| `snsf_brf_builder.py` | Billet reheating furnace (scaffolding)
| `btf_builder.py` | Box type furnace (scaffolding)
| `hpu_calculator.py` | Heating & pumping unit
| `burner_fabrication.py` | Burner fabrication items
|===
