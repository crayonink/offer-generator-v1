= Product Scope & Roadmap
:description: ENCON product-catalogue coverage and the offer-generation flow.

The Offer Generator's target scope is ENCON's full product catalogue
(the https://encon.co.in[encon.co.in] *Products* menu). This page shows
*how the product works* (offer-generation flow) and *what it covers*
(product map + coverage tables).

_Last updated: 2026-06-10_
