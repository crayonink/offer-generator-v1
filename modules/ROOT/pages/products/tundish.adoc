= Tundish Preheating System
:description: Inputs, outputs and calculation flow for the Tundish Preheating offer.

Module: `tundish`. Form: `/tundish` Ôćĺ `tundish_costing.html`. Status: Ôťů Complete. The tundish path *fully reuses the xref:products/vlph.adoc[VLPH] (ladle-preheater) pipeline*; this page documents what it shares and the three things it overrides.

== Inputs

The Tundish Preheating System reuses the VLPH request model `VLPHCalcRequest` (`main.py:636`) for sizing, plus the shared `QuoteRequest` model (`main.py:684`) for offer generation. The form is served at `GET /tundish` from `tundish_costing.html` (`main.py:788`). Tundish-specific behaviour is driven by `num_burners` and the two override fields below.

== Outputs

*BOM line items* ÔÇö returned by `POST /api/vlph-calculate` as `resp["bom"]` (`main.py:1681`), split into `MEDIA / ITEM NAME / REFERENCE / QTY / MAKE / UNIT PRICE / TOTAL`. Built by `build_vlph_120t_df` (automatic) or `build_vlph_manual_df` (manual) in `bom/vlph_builder.py`. Lines include: ENCON main burner (├Ś `num_burners`), blower, NG/MG gas train, AGR/control valve, butterfly + shut-off valves, gate valve, pressure gauges/switches, rotary joint, pilot burner & UV line, ceramic fibre, MS-structure fabrication, air-gas pipeline, and (for oil fuels) the HPU/Pumping Unit. Subtotals: `BOUGHT OUT ITEMS`, `ENCON ITEMS`, `GRAND TOTAL` (`main.py:1599ÔÇô1601`).

*Scope of supply / annexures* ÔÇö assembled in the offer docx by `engine/quote_writer.py`. Equipment name resolves to `"Tundish Preheater ÔÇô {tons} Ton"` via the `is_tundish` flag (`quote_writer.py:212,873`). Annexure I scope-of-supply, Annexure III price schedule, Annexure IV editable Terms & Conditions (`tnc_*` fields), and a MAKE LIST.

*Price schedule* ÔÇö the single equipment line carries `unit_price = final_total` (Grand + P&F + Designing + Negotiation + Transport, rounded to nearest Rs.1000; `tundish_costing.html:2330`). Transport is then split onto its own line by `_break_out_transport` (`main.py:4375`).

*Output .docx template* ÔÇö `generate_quote_docx` renders the tundish offer from the shared `Offer_Template.docx` (`quote_writer.py`): all products (Vertical, Horizontal, Tundish) share the same template shell, with `is_tundish` toggling tundish-only tech-data rows (`quote_writer.py:873,1215`) and scope wording. (The dormant `Tundish_Offer_Template.docx` / `Tundish_Automatic.docx` assets and the unused `TUNDISH_TEMPLATE_PATH` constant were removed.) The generated file is named `{date}_{Company}_Tundish-{N}T.docx` (`main.py:4551,4559`).

== Calculation Flow

The tundish path shares the VLPH calc endpoint `POST /api/vlph-calculate`, the burner/pipe/selector modules, both BOM builders, and the `Offer_Template.docx` renderer. It _overrides_ only three things: `num_burners` (per-burner sizing + line-item multiplication), `ms_structure_kg_override`, and `ceramic_rolls_override`.

. *Submit sizing inputs* ÔÇö The `/tundish` form posts to `POST /api/vlph-calculate` with `VLPHCalcRequest` (`main.py:1373`).
. *Compute total firing rate* ÔÇö In `direct` mode, `heat_kcal_hr = direct_burner_capacity ├Ś 860 ├Ś num_burners` (per-burner kW scaled to total), or the explicit `direct_heat_input_kcal_hr`; gas flow `ng_flow = heat/CV`, air `= heat ├Ś 118/100000` (`main.py:1396ÔÇô1403`). In `calc` mode, `calculate_burner(BurnerInputs(...))` (`calculations/burner.py:35`) returns `extra_firing_rate_nm3hr` and `air_qty_nm3hr` (`main.py:1407ÔÇô1416`).
. *Per-burner split (tundish override)* ÔÇö `n_burners = max(1, num_burners)`; `per_burner_ng = ng_flow/n` and `per_burner_air = air_flow/n` (`main.py:1426ÔÇô1428`). Blower/gas-train/main-line size off TOTAL flow; the burner sizes off PER-BURNER flow.
. *Dual-fuel air sizing* ÔÇö If dual, the blower is sized for whichever fuel needs more air (`blower_air_flow = max(air_flow, air_flow2)`), and oil LPH is precomputed for CFM sizing (`main.py:1432ÔÇô1456`).
. *Select equipment (total flow)* ÔÇö `select_equipment(...)` (`bom/selectors/selection_engine.py:20`) sizes pipes (source of truth for NB) then picks burner, blower, NG/MG gas train, AGR, control/butterfly/shut-off valves, rotary joint, air duct, and HPU ÔÇö each by next-bigger DB lookup against the computed flow (`main.py:1458ÔÇô1472`).
. *Size pipes* ÔÇö `calculate_pipe_sizes` after blower selection; air line sized from the blower's actual airflow (CFM ├Ś 1.7) per the air-line-NB rule (`main.py:1477ÔÇô1480`).
. *Re-select burner per-burner (tundish override)* ÔÇö When `n_burners > 1`, re-run `select_equipment` with `per_burner_ng/per_burner_air` and replace `equip1["burner"]` with the per-burner model (`main.py:1483ÔÇô1498`).
. *Fab / ceramic mapping with overrides (tundish)* ÔÇö Look up `fabrication_ladle_mapping`; `_ms_override = ms_structure_kg_override or mapped fabrication_kg`, `_ceramic_rolls = ceramic_rolls_override or mapped ceramic_rolls`; pipeline kg is always user-supplied (`main.py:1549ÔÇô1554`).
. *Build BOM with pricing* ÔÇö Automatic: `build_vlph_120t_df(... num_burners=n_burners, ms_structure_kg_override=_ms_override, ceramic_rolls_override=_ceramic_rolls ...)` (`main.py:1574`); Manual: `build_vlph_manual_df(...)` (`main.py:1558`). Each `_row` (`bom/vlph_builder.py:50`) prices via `unit_price_override` when supplied, otherwise exact-matches the DB `component_price_master` via `_get_price_fuzzy` (warns and prices 0 on no match ÔÇö see BOM zero-price audit).
. *Multiply per-burner BOM lines (tundish override)* ÔÇö When `num_burners > 1`, burner / ignition transformer / sequence controller / UV sensor / pilot-burner / pilot-line rows have `QTY` and `TOTAL` multiplied by `num_burners` (`bom/vlph_builder.py:1107ÔÇô1135`).
. *Subtotals* ÔÇö `bought_out_total`, `encon_total`, `grand_total` extracted from the df (`main.py:1599ÔÇô1601`); returned in `cost_summary`.
. *Commercial adjustments (client-side)* ÔÇö In `recalcGrandTotal()` (`tundish_costing.html:1975`): `grand = bought ├Ś markup + encon`; then ADD P&F, Designing, Negotiation (each `% of grand`) and flat Transport; round the Final Total to nearest Rs.1000 (`tundish_costing.html:1990`).
. *Generate offer* ÔÇö `POST /api/generate-quote` with `QuoteRequest` (`main.py:4445,684`), `product_type = "Tundish Preheater"`, `unit_price = final_total` (`tundish_costing.html:2324ÔÇô2331`). `calculate_quote` Ôćĺ `generate_quote_docx` renders `Offer_Template.docx` with `is_tundish=True` (`quote_writer.py:873`, `main.py:4561`).
. *Post-process docx* ÔÇö `_break_out_transport` splits the flat Transport amount onto its own price-schedule line (`main.py:4375,4565`); empty tech rows stripped, MAKE LIST appended, scope columns pruned (`quote_writer.py:1058ÔÇô1072`); PDF rendered via LibreOffice (`main.py:4573`).
