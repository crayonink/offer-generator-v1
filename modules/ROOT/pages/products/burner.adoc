= Burners
:description: What a standalone Burner offer needs, what it produces, and how its price is worked out — in plain language.

A burner mixes fuel and air and provides the flame that heats a furnace or ladle. This offer is for selling a burner on its own, separate from a full preheater. Like the blower offer, it is a "pick from the catalogue" offer — you choose a pre-priced burner and the system prices it.

== What you enter

* *Burner type* — ENCON oil burner, ENCON gas burner, ENCON dual-fuel burner, or a GAIL pre-assembled gas set.
* *Burner model* — the specific size, chosen from a drop-down for the selected type.
* *Quantity* — how many burners.
* *Commercial figures* — packaging & forwarding percentage, designing percentage, negotiation percentage, and a flat transport amount.
* *Customer details and Terms & Conditions.*

The fuel, the firing capacity and the unit price are filled in automatically from the catalogue once you pick a model — you don't type them in. (Firing capacity is shown in litres per hour for ENCON sizes, or in kilowatts for GAIL sets.)

== What the offer contains

The offer document (Word + PDF) includes:

* *A short scope paragraph* — "we will supply N burner(s) of capacity … to achieve the desired temperature", followed by the main components.
* *A bulleted list* of what makes up the burner package: the burner, a ball valve, a CI mounting plate, a burner block, a set of flexible hoses, and a butterfly valve.
* *A price schedule* with quantity, unit price and total; transport, if entered, appears on its own line.
* *The grand total in figures and words*, and the standard Terms & Conditions.

A running total updates live as you change the quantity or commercial figures, and the finished offer can be previewed before sending.

== How the price is worked out

. *Look up the chosen burner* in the catalogue and take its price.
. *Multiply by the quantity.*
. *Add the commercials* — packaging & forwarding, designing and negotiation are each added as a percentage (they add to the price, they do not discount it), and the flat transport is added.
. *Round* the total to the nearest ₹1,000 when any commercial figure has been applied.
. *Produce the documents* — the price goes into the offer, transport is shown on its own line, and the Word file and PDF are created and filed.

NOTE: This standalone offer is purely a priced catalogue pick. It does not perform any firing-rate or fabrication calculation, and it does not add a pilot burner — those belong to the full preheater offers, where the burner is part of a larger system.
