= Blowers
:description: What a standalone Blower offer needs, what it produces, and how its price is worked out — in plain language.

A blower supplies combustion air to a burner. This offer is for selling a blower on its own (with its motor and base frame), separate from a full preheater. It is a straightforward "pick from the catalogue" offer — you choose a blower model and the system prices it.

== What you enter

* *Customer details* — company, address, contacts and the subject of the offer.
* *Blower model* — chosen from a drop-down list of catalogue blowers. Each model already carries its own horsepower, airflow and pressure rating, so there is nothing to size by hand.
* *Quantity* — how many blower sets.
* *Commercial figures* — packaging & forwarding percentage, designing percentage, negotiation percentage, and a flat transport amount.

The introduction paragraph and the bullet list of what's supplied are written automatically from the model you pick.

== What the offer contains

The offer document (Word + PDF) includes:

* *A short scope paragraph* describing the blower — a centrifugal steel-plate air blower of the chosen horsepower and airflow, with a suitable electric motor on a common base frame.
* *A bulleted list* of what's supplied: the blower, the drive motor, and a common base frame with anti-vibration pads.
* *A price schedule* with the quantity, unit price and total. If a transport amount was entered, it appears on its own separate line.
* *The grand total in figures and words*, and the standard Terms & Conditions.

While filling in the form, a running total updates live as you change the quantity or any commercial figure, and after the offer is generated you can preview it before sending.

== How the price is worked out

. *Look up the chosen blower* in the catalogue and take its standard price (the price including the motor).
. *Multiply by the quantity.*
. *Add the commercials* — packaging & forwarding, designing and negotiation are each added as a percentage (they add to the price, they do not discount it), and the flat transport is added.
. *Round* the total to the nearest ₹1,000 when any commercial figure has been applied.
. *Produce the documents* — the price goes into the offer, transport is shown on its own line, and the Word file and PDF are created and filed.

NOTE: This standalone offer simply supplies the blower, motor and base frame — it does not size any air pipework. The air-line sizing rule (sized from the blower's airflow) only applies inside the full preheater offers.
