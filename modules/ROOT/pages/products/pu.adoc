= Pumping Unit (PU-only)
:description: What a Pumping Unit offer needs, what it produces, and how its price is worked out — in plain language.

A pumping unit pumps fuel oil to the burner at the right pressure. It is the same skid as the xref:products/hpu.adoc[Heating & Pumping Unit] but *without the heater* — used for oils that are already hot enough (or heated elsewhere) and only need pumping.

== What you enter

[cols="3,4"]
|===
|What you enter |Typical value / options

|Variant |Simplex (single), Duplex 1 (default), or Duplex 2 (with standby)
|Capacity |9 kW (choices: 3 … 48)
|Oil flow rate |filled in automatically from the capacity and variant
|Quantity |1 (number of units)
|Packaging & forwarding |5 %
|Designing |10 %
|Negotiation |0 %
|Transport |flat ₹ amount (default 0)
|Customer details and Terms & Conditions |company, contacts, subject, and the standard terms
|===

There are no heating, combustion or burner settings — the pumping unit is just the oil-pumping skid.

== What the offer contains

The offer document (Word + PDF) includes:

* *A scope section headed "Pumping Unit"* describing a pumping skid of the chosen variant, with bullets listing:
** the pump(s), each with its electric motor;
** duplex coarse and fine filters (cold and hot side);
** a pressure-regulating valve;
** a pressure gauge.
+
Compared with the Heating & Pumping Unit, there is *no oil pre-heater and no temperature gauge* — that is the main difference between the two products.
* *A price schedule* with a single all-inclusive line; transport on its own line; packaging & forwarding, designing and negotiation built into the unit price.
* *The grand total* in figures and words.
* *The Terms & Conditions.*

== How the price is worked out

. *Look up the oil flow rate* for the chosen capacity and variant.
. *Take the unit price* straight from the catalogue. (Unlike the Heating & Pumping Unit, the price is read directly — it already allows for the mark-up, and there is no heater to add.)
. *Multiply by the quantity.*
. *Add the commercials* — packaging & forwarding, designing and negotiation added as percentages (they add to the price, they do not discount it), plus flat transport.
. *Round* the total to the nearest ₹1,000 when any commercial figure has been applied.
. *Produce the documents* — the all-inclusive price goes into the offer, transport is shown on its own line, and the Word file and PDF are created and filed.

[NOTE]
.Worked example — default unit
====
Chosen unit = *9 kW, Duplex 1* (model PUD-9, oil flow 120 LPH). Its catalogue price (already mark-up-included, no heater) = *₹1,13,798*.

* Quantity 1, P&F 5 % + designing 10 % + negotiation 0 % (= +15 %): 1,13,798 × 1.15 = 1,30,867 → rounded to the nearest ₹1,000 = *₹1,31,000*

Compare the xref:products/hpu.adoc[Heating & Pumping Unit] — the same 9 kW Duplex 1 unit there is *₹1,19,326* (final ₹1,37,000) because it adds an oil pre-heater and temperature gauge. The pumping unit is *₹5,528 cheaper* at the unit-price level for every size.
====

== Full calculation reference (every step)

A pumping unit offer is a *catalogue pick* — the same pump skid as the xref:products/hpu.adoc[Heating & Pumping Unit] but with the *oil pre-heater and temperature gauge removed*. This section documents every step with the real catalogue figures.

=== The three variants

[cols="2,4",options="header"]
|===
| Variant | What's in it
| *Simplex* (model PUS-) | One pump + motor — no standby
| *Duplex 1* (model PUD-, default) | *Two* pumps + motors (one running, one standby)
| *Duplex 2* (model PUDD-) | Two pumps + motors with extra redundancy
|===

Every variant has duplex coarse and fine filters, a pressure-regulating valve and a pressure gauge. None has a heater or temperature gauge — that is the whole difference from the heating-and-pumping unit.

=== Pricing (standalone offer)

[cols="3,3,2",options="header"]
|===
| Step | Formula | Worked
| Unit price | catalogue price (mark-up already included) | ₹1,13,798 (9 kW Duplex 1)
| Line total | unit × quantity | × 1 = ₹1,13,798
| Commercials | × (1 + P&F% + Designing% + Negotiation%) | × 1.15 = ₹1,30,867
| Final | round to nearest ₹1,000 (when any commercial applied) | *₹1,31,000*
|===

NOTE: There are no heating, combustion or burner settings — the pumping unit is just the oil-pumping skid. P&F, designing and negotiation are all *added* (negotiation does not discount); transport is added to the total and then shown on its own line.
