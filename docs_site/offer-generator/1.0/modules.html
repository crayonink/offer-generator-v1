Modules & Equipment

For per-product build status see Product Scope & Roadmap.

Calculation layer (calculations/)

  • pipes.py — gas & combustion-air pipe sizing (round_up_to_nb, velocities).

  • burner.py — burner firing-rate / heat-load calculations.

  • recup.py — recuperator heat-transfer & surface-area sizing.

  • regen.py — regenerative burner sizing.

Selection engine (bom/selectors/)

selection_engine.py orchestrates catalog-part selection (burner, gas train, AGR, blower, air duct, butterfly valve, rotary joint, HPU). Individual selectors: encon_burner, ng_gas_train, agr_selector, blower_selector, air_duct_selector, air_valve_selector, rotary_joint_selector, orifice_plate_selector, gas_regulator_selector, hpu_selector.

BOM builders (bom/*_builder.py)

Each builder turns selected equipment + parameters into priced _row(…​) line items tagged with MEDIA, ITEM NAME, REFERENCE, QTY, MAKE, UNIT PRICE, TOTAL.
