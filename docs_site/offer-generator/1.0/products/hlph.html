Horizontal Ladle Preheater (HLPH)

Module: hlph. Form: /hlph → hlph_costing.html. Status: ✅ Complete. Shares the ladle-preheater pipeline with the Vertical Ladle Preheater; this page focuses on the horizontal differences.

Inputs

The HLPH form is served at /hlph (main.py:782). Step-2 calculation posts a VLPHCalcRequest (main.py:636) to POST /api/hlph-calculate (main.py:2055). Commercial inputs (markup, P&F, designing, negotiation, transport) are not part of VLPHCalcRequest — they live only in the form’s grand-total recalc (hlph_costing.html:1940) and are baked into the offer’s single line price at generate time.

Outputs

POST /api/hlph-calculate returns calculations, pipes, equipment, bom (line items), and cost_summary (bought_out_total / encon_total / grand_total). The BOM is built by bom/hlph_builder.py — build_hlph_df (automatic, line 25) or build_hlph_manual_df (manual, line 314), selected at main.py:2187.

BOM line items (automatic mode)

  • ENCON ITEMS: FABRICATION/STRUCTURE (ms_kg × FABRICATION RATE), AIR-GAS PIPELINE (pipeline_kg × PIPELINE RATE), CERAMIC FIBRE (rolls @ DB rate), the ENCON burner (or "ENCON DUAL FUEL Burner"), blower, and — for oil fuels — the Heating & Pumping Unit (HPU).

  • Trolley drive group (HLPH-specific): GEARED MOTOR MECHANISM (3 HP, POWERTEK), TROLLEY WHEEL ×4, PLUMMER BLOCK ×6, SHAFT (1 long, 2 short), BEARING (TROLLEY) ×4, SLIEVE ×4, FRAME (MS Structure) (hlph_builder.py:86–94).

  • COMB AIR: COMPENSATOR, PRESSURE GAUGE WITH TNV, PRESSURE SWITCH LOW; plus (PLC) ORIFICE PLATE + DPT; plus (PLC/PLC+AGR/PID/PPID-ratio) CONTROL VALVE + BUTTERFLY VALVE.

  • Fuel 1 / Fuel 2 lines: via shared _fuel_line_rows (gas train, AGR, shutoff/ball/solenoid valves, regulator, hoses).

  • Optional: PURGING LINE (6 rows, MG/COG); PILOT LINE + pilot/UV ancillaries + ignition equipment (Ignition Transformer, Sequence Controller, UV Sensor, Pilot Burner) only when special_auto_ignition is true.

  • MISC / static items: from bom.static_items() minus a STATIC_SKIP set, then CONTROL PANEL, INSTRUMENTS BALL VALVE ×3, and (PLC modes) PLC WITH HMI.

  • Trailer rows: BOUGHT OUT ITEMS, ENCON ITEMS, GRAND TOTAL subtotals.

HLPH-specific vs VLPH

  • No ROTARY JOINT. selection_engine.select_equipment still selects a rotary joint (shared with VLPH), but hlph_builder never emits that row — horizontal has no swirling/rotary mechanism (hlph_builder.py:132).

  • Has the trolley drive group (geared motor, wheels, plummer blocks, shaft, bearings, sleeve, frame) in place of VLPH’s swirling-mechanism / hood rows.

  • Sizing from get_hlph_params → horizontal_master DB table (ladle_params.py:220), which carries a trolley_drive_cost and a separate pipeline_cost (no swirling), unlike VLPH’s pipeline_swirling_cost.

  • Pipeline weight always user-supplied (DB has no horizontal pipeline data; main.py:2182).

  • STATIC_SKIP behaves the same as VLPH: always skips CONTROL PANEL and HYDRAULIC POWER PACK & CYLINDER; PLC/PLC+AGR additionally skip PPID and RATIO CONTROLLER; PID skips TEMPERATURE TRANSMITTER and RATIO CONTROLLER (and renames PPID→PID); PPID-ratio skips only TEMPERATURE TRANSMITTER (hlph_builder.py:257–273).

Annexures / scope-of-supply

The offer .docx (engine/quote_writer.py) renders Annexure I scope-of-supply, Annexure III price schedule, and Annexure IV terms. Scope bullets come from bom_items posted from the BOM; horizontal_qty feeds the Annexure I header count.

Price schedule & commercial adjustments

Per recalcGrandTotal (hlph_costing.html:1940): Grand = bought × markup + encon, then Final = round1000( Grand + Grand·P&F% + Grand·Design% + Grand·Neg% + Transport ). Negotiation adds (its % field is applied positively). The Final Total is posted as the single equipment line unit_price/total (hlph_costing.html:2395). Transport is then split onto its own Annexure III line by _break_out_transport (main.py:4375), leaving TOTAL unchanged.

Output template

generate_quote_docx uses the shared TEMPLATE_PATH = Offer_Template.docx (quote_writer.py:5) — HLPH and VLPH share one template. Filename: {YYYY-MM-DD}_{Customer}_HLPH-{tons}T.docx (main.py:4545–4559), with a parallel .pdf (LibreOffice render) and .json snapshot.

Calculation Flow

  1. Heat / flow (calc mode): calculate_burner (calculations/burner.py:35) computes avg temp rise (delta formula when Ti>100), firing rate = weight·factor·Δtemp, heat load = firing/efficiency, fuel consumption, ×1.1 → extra_firing_rate_nm3hr (= ng_flow), and air qty = cv·extra·118/100000. Direct mode: heat_kcal = capacity×860 (or explicit kcal); ng_flow = heat/cv, air = heat·118/100000 (main.py:2073–2093).

  2. Burner WG: 36 if blower_pressure=="40" else 24 (main.py:2095).

  3. Dual-fuel blower air: blower sized for max(air1, air2); oil fuel-2 LPH pre-computed for CFM (main.py:2097–2121).

  4. Equipment selection: select_equipment (selection_engine.py:20) — pipe NB from calculate_pipe_sizes; burner from select_encon_mg_burner (by gas flow + CV + fuel category); NG gas train (select_ng_gas_train); AGR only when manual or auto∈{plc_agr,pid}, sized from gas-train outlet NB; blower HP = CFM × pressure / 3200 where gas_cfm = air/1.7, oil_cfm = LPH×10, cfm = max(…​); air duct; combustion-air line NB = max(125, pipe-NB from blower CFM×1.7, air_duct NB) (selection_engine.py:104–111, per the "air line = blower CFM × 1.7" rule); butterfly valve (lever→gear fallback); rotary joint (selected but unused by HLPH); HPU/Pumping Unit for oil fuels.

  5. Pipe sizing: calculate_pipe_sizes (calculations/pipes.py:67) — gas NB at 12.7 m/s, air NB at 15.0 m/s, rounded up to STANDARD_PIPE_NB. Oil fuel-2 line NB via select_oil_pipe_nb (≤250 LPH→20 NB else 25). Re-run for fuel 1 using the burner’s actual air demand (main.py:2136).

  6. Sizing-row auto-fill: lookup_ladle_fab_pipeline(ladle_tons, "horizontal") maps the nearest horizontal row; ms_structure_kg_override and ceramic_rolls_override win when nonzero, else DB-mapped; pipeline weight is always the user value (main.py:2180–2185).

  7. BOM build: build_hlph_df (or build_hlph_manual_df if control_mode=="manual") (main.py:2187–2214). get_hlph_params (ladle_params.py:220) supplies fabrication kg, ceramic rolls/rate, pipeline & trolley costs from horizontal_master.

  8. Row pricing: _row either uses unit_price_override (fabrication = kg × FABRICATION RATE, pipeline = kg × PIPELINE RATE, ceramic = rolls × ceramic_rate, burner/blower/HPU = selector price) or falls back to a DB lookup by exact item name. Per the BOM zero-price caveat, any row without an override prices 0 if its name ≠ a DB key.

  9. Subtotals: bought_out_total = Σ TOTAL where MEDIA≠"ENCON ITEMS"; encon_total = Σ ENCON ITEMS; grand_total = sum. Returned in cost_summary (hlph_builder.py:290–309).

  10. Commercial adjustments (form): recalcGrandTotal — Grand = bought×markup + encon; Final = round-to-₹1000( Grand + P&F% + Design% + Negotiation%(ADD) + Transport ) (hlph_costing.html:1940–1959).

  11. Cost variations (optional): POST /api/hlph-cost-variations (main.py:2352) re-runs the calc across single-axis swaps (vendors, auto-ignition, HPU variant for oil, auto-control type, control-mode flip). No hood axis.

  12. Offer generation: form posts QuoteRequest (main.py:684) with the single HLPH line at unit_price = Final Total → POST /api/generate-quote (main.py:4445) → calculate_quote then generate_quote_docx against Offer_Template.docx → _break_out_transport splits the transport line → PDF (LibreOffice) + JSON snapshot + Drive upload + quotes_log insert.
